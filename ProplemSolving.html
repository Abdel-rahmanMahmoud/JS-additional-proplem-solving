Problem solving is a process of identifying a problem, analyzing it, and finding a solution. It involves critical thinking, creativity, and the ability to evaluate different options.

Steps in Problem Solving

  1. Identify the problem
  2. Analyze the problem
  3. Generate possible solutions
  4. Evaluate the solutions
  5. Implement the solution
  6. Review the results
